ASHENFALL
The Ruined Watch-Circle — VR Combat Trial
Three foes hold the ruined circle: a stone Warden, a swarm of Wraiths, and a lurking Crag Spider. Fight with steel, arrow, and three schools of magic — and open your pack to swap weapons and armor mid-battle.
Requires a secure (https) connection and a WebXR-capable browser — the Meta Quest Browser works out of the box.
Right hand: swing to strike  ·  Right grip: cast spell  ·  Right thumbstick click: cycle spell
Left trigger: loose an arrow  ·  Left grip: open/close inventory  ·  Point right hand + trigger to select
